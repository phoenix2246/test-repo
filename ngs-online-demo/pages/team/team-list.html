We see ourselves as much more than just a studio, but as a family of like-minded, kind and talented people who love to collaborate and make awesome sh*t together.

We’re definitely close-knit, but not in a weird, creepy kind of way. We genuinely like each other and like to spend time together, whether it's for a quick cappuccino break in the studio or a night out at the latest gallery or museum opening.

While we love what we do, we know that life is about more than work and that we each have one beyond Bolden. We value healthy boundaries and will never ask you to work late or bother you on weekends (unless it’s for something fun).

Expecting a delivery and need to work from home? Kid’s sick and need to jet? No problem! We’ve always had a flexible approach to working from home and totally understand if you need to stay in or things come up. Life happens.
